This Website is to learn about the most chill animal

The capybarais a giant cavy rodent native to South America. Its close relatives include guinea pigs and rock cavies,. The capybara inhabits savannas and dense forests and lives near bodies of water. It is a highly social species and can be found in groups as large as 100 individuals, but usually lives in groups of 10–20 individuals.
